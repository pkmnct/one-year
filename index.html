A Year in Life

What has happened in one year?

A couple of pretty cool people (George and Alex) met in December 2015.

In March 2016, they caucused for the first time.

That month George practiced portrait photography with Alex, too.

Come April, they explored Tuttle Creek with friends.

They went to Little Apple Pride.

Alex went to his first Drag Show.

Then, in August, he moved into his first apartment.

That month, George dyed his hair (for the first time) pink.

They befriended a Kitter.

In October, they went to see the Rocky Horror Picture Show.

Then they saw Troye Sivan in concert.

In November, George had his first facemask.

Finally, in December 2016, they realized they'd been together a year.

Troye Sivan: for him.

This site was created by George Walker to celebrate a year with Alex.
